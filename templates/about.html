About LungPredict

Our Mission

LungPredict is a cutting-edge medical AI tool designed to assist healthcare professionals in quickly and accurately diagnosing respiratory conditions from chest X-ray images. Using advanced deep learning technology, our system can identify patterns associated with COVID-19, Viral Pneumonia, and normal lung conditions.

Our mission is to make advanced medical diagnostics more accessible, efficient, and accurate to save lives through early detection and timely intervention.

Our Technology

EfficientNetB0 Neural Network

LungPredict uses Google's EfficientNetB0 architecture, a state-of-the-art convolutional neural network (CNN) specifically optimized for both accuracy and computational efficiency. This advanced model has been meticulously trained on thousands of verified chest X-ray images.

  • Optimized to achieve high accuracy with fewer parameters
  • Utilizes compound scaling for balanced depth, width, and resolution
  • Enhanced feature extraction capabilities for medical imaging
  • Transfer learning from ImageNet weights for improved performance
  • Fine-tuned specifically for respiratory disease identification

How It Works

  1. Upload a chest X-ray image through our secure interface
  2. Our EfficientNetB0 model processes the image, extracting key features
  3. The deep learning algorithm analyzes the patterns against trained datasets
  4. Results are displayed showing prediction class and confidence level
  5. All predictions are securely stored for future reference by healthcare providers
